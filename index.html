About

  • Tel Aviv's bicycle landscape faces challenges such as parking scarcity, expenses, and environmental pollution.
  • Misuse of bike racks leads to abandoned bicycles cluttering the city streets.
  • Our solution: Paid private bicycle parking, maximizing space utilization and promoting cycling culture.

Services

Mobile App

Provides users with a convenient solution for urban bike parking. Designed to combat the issue of bikes being parked to rot in urban areas, by making users pay for their parking by the hour.

🌐 User-Friendly Interface

The app has an intuitive and user-friendly interface that allows users to quickly sign up, locate nearby stations, and park their bike with ease.

🗺️ Station Locator

The app displays a map showing the users current location as well as nearby stations. Users can get relevant information about each station, including hourly rates, available locks, and more.

📷 QR Code Scanning

Users can scan the QR code on the lock to start and end a parking rental.

⏱️ Control of Current Rentals

Users have control over their ongoing rentals through the app. They can view rental details and remotely unlock the slot.

🚴‍♂️ Riding History

The app maintains a record of users' past rentals, allowing them to review their rental history.

🔐 Authentication and Security

The app ensures data integrity and authenticity through authentication measures and encrypted communication.

🌎 Global Availability

The app is available on mobile for authorized users.

Services

Manager Dashboard

This platform is designed to empower cities and operators in managing the lock rental services, enhancing the urban mobility landscape.

📊 Comprehensive Management

Our web app provides a centralized hub for overseeing all aspects of the lock rentals. Operators can effortlessly monitor stations, locks, and rentals by the information provided via our intuitive dashboards.

💳 Customizable Payment Policies

Our web app provides managers with an interface for changing the hourly rate of each of their stations, which take effect for all new rentals while old rentals rates remain unchanged.

🔒 Adding/Deleting New Items

Allows for the expansion/removal of offerings by adding/deleting stations and locks to the eco-system.

🔄 Deploying Item Changes

Deleted items disappear from the eco-system as soon as the are freed of use. Support the deployment of new items by providing operators with the relevant data required for deploying the relevant programs to the physical devices almost seamlessly.

Services

BLE Technology and Flask Integration Arduino

⚙️ Arduino-based Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE)

Ensures secure communication and control through HMAC-based authentication and an LED indicator. This setup establishes a safe connection, verifies responses and enables remote control via BLE.

🌐 Gateway

Involves a Python program that integrates Flask and Bleak libraries to facilitate a BLE lock rental system. This solution: - ensures secure rental requests via HMAC - facilitates BLE lock communication - authorizes locking/unlocking actions These solutions prioritize security, offering pioneering means to enhance control and rental procedures using BLE technology and advanced cryptography.
